The Keyob story

For thirty years we built the engines. Now we're teaching them to think.

Keyob didn't arrive with the AI wave — we were already here. For more than three decades we've done the unglamorous, decisive work: business analysis, discovery, and the design and maintenance of full ERP systems for auto parts distributors, real estate groups, health networks, and construction firms.

That history matters. You cannot make a business intelligent until you understand exactly how it runs. We've spent careers learning that — mapping the workflows, the data, the decisions that hold up an organisation.

Nearly a decade ago, we started building AI directly into the systems we'd designed. Today we go further: we bridge the tools you already use — the CRMs, the spreadsheets, the over-the-counter software you don't want to abandon — and connect them into one flexible, automated, intelligent whole. We modernise without the rebuild.
